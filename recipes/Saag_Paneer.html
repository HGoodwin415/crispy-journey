Authentic Saag Paneer


Saag paneer is a classic Indian dish of cooked spinach studded with cubes of fried paneer cheese. Thickened with cream or coconut milk

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches pf spinach, roughly chopped
  • 1 bunch fenugreek leaves, roughly chopped
  • 3 Tbs of canola oil, divide
  • 1/2 pounds of paneer, cubed
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 2 tps garam masala
  • 1/2 tps ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tps cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Gather all your ingridents
  2. Bring a large saucepan of water to boil: add spinach and fengugreek and cook until wilted, about 3 mins. Drain well.
  3. Transfer to a food processor and blend until finely chopped, about 5 pulses.
  4. Heat 1 Tbs canola oil in a large skillet over medium heat; add paneer cubes and fry until browned on all sides, about 5 mins. Transfer panneer onto a plate.
  5. Heat remaining 2 Tbs canola oil in the same skillet over medium heat; add cumin seeds and fry until lightly toasted and aromatic, about 3 minutes.
  6. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, 4 to 5 mins. Add garlic and ginger and stir to coat.
  7. Stir in tomato, garam masala, turmeric, and cayenne pepper; cook until tomato breaks down, stirring often, about 10 mins.
  8. Stir in pureed spinach mixture, paneer cubes, and cream; add salt to taste.
  9. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 mins, stirring occasionally.
  10. Serve with Naan bread, optional.
